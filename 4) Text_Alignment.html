Left Alignment

HTML, or Hypertext Markup Language, is the backbone of the internet, defining the structure of web content through a system of tags. These tags encapsulate elements such as headings, paragraphs, and links, enabling developers to organize information logically. HTML's significance extends to ensuring cross-browser compatibility and facilitating accessibility. It serves as the foundation for building visually appealing and interactive websites when coupled with CSS and JavaScript. With its simplicity and adherence to web standards, HTML stands as an essential language for front-end developers, empowering them to craft engaging and well-structured web experiences for users worldwide.
